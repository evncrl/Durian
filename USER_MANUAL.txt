1. GETTING STARTED - LOGIN & REGISTRATION
================================================================================

CREATING A NEW ACCOUNT (Registration)
--------------------------------------

Step 1: On the main login screen, tap the "Create Account" or "Sign Up" button.

Step 2: Fill in your registration information:
        - Email Address: Enter a valid email you have access to
        - Password: Create a strong password (mix of letters, numbers, symbols)
        - Confirm Password: Re-enter your password to confirm
        - Full Name: Enter your first and last name
        - Phone Number (Optional): Add your contact number if desired

Step 3: Review the Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy.

Step 4: Tap "Register" or "Create Account" to complete registration.

Step 5: A verification email will be sent to your email address.
        Open your email and click the verification link.

Step 6: Your account is now active! You can log in with your email and password.


LOGGING INTO YOUR ACCOUNT
--------------------------

Step 1: On the main screen, tap the login field.

Step 2: Enter your email address and password.

Step 3: Tap the "Log In" button.

Step 4: You're now logged in! You'll be taken to the home screen.


RESETTING A FORGOTTEN PASSWORD
-------------------------------

Step 1: On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?"

Step 2: Enter your registered email address.

Step 3: Tap "Send Reset Link"

Step 4: Check your email for a password reset link.

Step 5: Click the link and set a new password.

Step 6: Log in with your new password.


LOGGING OUT
-----------

Step 1: Go to your Profile section (usually bottom menu or settings).

Step 2: Scroll down and tap "Log Out"

Step 3: You'll be returned to the login screen.

2. USING THE SHOP FEATURE
================================================================================

BROWSING PRODUCTS
-----------------

Step 1: From the home screen, tap the "Shop" tab or icon.

Step 2: You'll see a list of available durian products and items.

Step 3: Use the category filters at the top to narrow down products:
        - Filter by type (Fresh Durians, Processed, Frozen, etc.)
        - Sort by price, rating, or newest arrivals

Step 4: Tap on any product to view more details:
        - Product description
        - Price
        - Availability
        - Customer ratings and reviews
        - Product images


ADDING ITEMS TO YOUR CART
-------------------------

Step 1: Select a product you want to purchase.

Step 2: Choose your quantity using the "+" and "-" buttons.

Step 3: Select any options if available (size, variant, etc.).

Step 4: Tap "Add to Cart" button.

Step 5: You'll see a confirmation message.


VIEWING YOUR CART
-----------------

Step 1: Look for the shopping cart icon (usually at the top right).

Step 2: Tap to view all items in your cart.

Step 3: Review quantities and prices for each item.

Step 4: Use "+" and "-" buttons to adjust quantities, or swipe to remove items.


PROCEEDING TO CHECKOUT
----------------------

Step 1: From your cart, tap "Proceed to Checkout"

Step 2: Verify your shipping address:
        - Add or select an existing address
        - Make sure all details are correct

Step 3: Select your shipping method:
        - Standard Shipping (5-7 days)
        - Express Shipping (2-3 days)
        - Next Day Delivery

Step 4: Review your order summary:
        - Items and quantities
        - Shipping cost
        - Total amount due

Step 5: Select your payment method:
        - Credit/Debit Card
        - Mobile Wallet
        - Bank Transfer
        - Other available options

Step 6: Complete the payment process.

Step 7: You'll receive an order confirmation and tracking number.


TRACKING YOUR ORDER
-------------------

Step 1: Go to your Profile section.

Step 2: Tap "My Orders" or "Order History"

Step 3: Select the order you want to track.

Step 4: View real-time tracking information:
        - Current location of your package
        - Estimated delivery date
        - Delivery status

================================================================================

ACCESSING THE CHATBOT
---------------------

Step 1: Look for the chat icon or "Chatbot" option in the app menu.

Step 2: Tap to open the chat interface.

Step 3: You'll see a chat window with our AI assistant.


ASKING QUESTIONS
----------------

Step 1: Type your question in the message box at the bottom.

----------------

- Quick Replies: The chatbot may offer suggested responses. Tap any of them
  for quick answers.

- Search Information: The chatbot can provide information about durian varieties,
  nutrition, storage, recipes, and more.

- Product Assistance: Ask about available products, prices, and recommendations.

- Help: If you need general help, type "help" or "menu" to see available options.


ENDING A CHAT
-------------

Step 1: Tap the "X" button to close the chat window.

Step 2: Your conversation will be saved (if feature is enabled).

Step 3: You can return and continue chatting anytime.

5. USING THE SCANNER
================================================================================

ACCESSING THE SCANNER
---------------------

Step 1: From the home menu, tap the "Scanner" tab or camera icon.

Step 2: Grant camera permissions if prompted (needed to use the camera).

Step 3: The scanner interface will open showing your device's camera view.


SCANNING A DURIAN
-----------------

Step 1: Hold your device steady and point the camera at a durian.

Step 2: Make sure the durian is:
        - Well-lit (good lighting is important)
        - Clearly visible in the camera frame
        - Fully or mostly within the frame

Step 3: Tap the "Capture" or "Scan" button.

Step 4: The AI will analyze the durian and process the image.


UNDERSTANDING YOUR SCAN RESULTS
--------------------------------

After scanning, you'll receive a detailed analysis including:

1. DURIAN VARIETY/TYPE
   - The identified species and variety
   - Confidence level of the identification

2. RIPENESS LEVEL
   - Unripe, Semi-ripe, Ripe, or Over-ripe status
   - Visual indicators (color, texture)

3. QUALITY ASSESSMENT
   - Overall quality score
   - Grade (Premium, Standard, Lower Grade)

4. HEALTH/DISEASE DETECTION
   - Indicators of any diseases or defects
   - Warnings if detected
   - Safe to eat determinations

5. SHAPE & SIZE ANALYSIS
   - Shape classification
   - Estimated size
   - Physical characteristics

6. COLOR ANALYSIS
   - Color profile and gradation
   - Natural color patterns
   - Ripeness indicators from color


VIEWING SCAN HISTORY
--------------------

Step 1: In the Scanner section, look for "History" or "Past Scans"

Step 2: You'll see a list of all previous scans with:
        - Date and time of scan
        - Product identified
        - Scan results
        - Thumbnail image

Step 3: Tap any previous scan to view detailed results again.


SCANNER TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS
-----------------------------

- LIGHTING: Use natural or bright artificial light for best accuracy.
  Avoid shadows on the durian.

- ANGLE: Take photos from multiple angles for better accuracy.

- DISTANCE: Hold the camera 20-30 cm away from the durian.

- FOCUS: Make sure the image is sharp and in focus before scanning.

- STEADINESS: Keep your hand steady or use a stand to prevent blurring.

- BACKGROUND: Use a neutral background to make the durian stand out.

6. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS & TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

Q: What if I forget my password?
A: Go to the login screen and tap "Forgot Password?" Follow the instructions
   to reset it via email.

Q: How long does delivery take?
A: Standard delivery is 5-7 business days, Express is 2-3 days, and Next Day
   is available in select areas. Exact times vary by location.

Q: Can I return an order?
A: Yes! Go to "My Orders" in your Profile, select the order, and tap "Return
   This Order." Follow the return process. Most returns are accepted within
   30 days.

Q: Is my personal information safe?
A: Yes! We use encryption and follow strict privacy policies. Your data is
   never shared with third parties without consent.

Q: The scanner isn't recognizing my durian. What should I do?
A: Ensure good lighting, clear focus, and that the entire durian is visible.
   Try taking multiple photos from different angles. The AI works best with
   clear, well-lit images.

Q: How do I become a forum moderator?
A: Active community members with high reputation scores may be invited to
   become moderators. There's no direct application process.

Q: Can I edit or delete my forum posts?
A: Yes! You can edit posts within 24 hours of posting. Click the three-dot
   menu next to your post and select "Edit" or "Delete."

Q: I have a payment issue. How do I get help?
A: Contact our support team through the Settings/Help section of the app,
   or email our customer service team.

Q: Can I chat with the chatbot about topics other than durians?
A: The chatbot is optimized for durian-related questions. It may handle
   general questions, but works best with product and durian-specific queries.

Q: Why was my forum post removed?
A: Posts may be removed if they violate community guidelines (spam, abuse,
   inappropriate content). Check your email for details from the moderators.
